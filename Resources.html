The Project Description of the group HSE33 is
Help the community of Alangilan, Batangas to help solve their waste management issues. Our project will be a website about Alangilan, its waste management issues, and how we can all help in improving and removing the land pollution in their area.

The group's Desired Outcome is
A website that can encourage everyone to do proper waste garbage disposal in their own home. The group would present possibilities that can happen if they will not do proper waste disposal at home. The group would also present weekly updates on the website. The updates in the website will be from someone in the targeted community.

Project Overview
1.Specific Tasks to be Taken
1.1The group will improve the plan for Implementation.
1.2The group will plan our budget for our expenses and website creation.
1.3The group will create an interesting website with regular updates on the local garbage management practices and their effects. Promotional initiatives and ongoing oversight will help to enhance its impact and reach.

2.People in-Charge
2.1Members of HSE33 are in charge to accomplish each task
2.2Members of HSE 33 and the community will be involved to allow us to hear and discuss all the opinions that everyone will share/offer
2.3Members of HSE 33 will be a part of it to contribute in some way, whether it’s the web design or a part of the presentation. Everyone’s voices in the group will be heard, everyone’s ideas will be discussed.

3.Resources Needed
3.1We require landfills and garbage bins for proper waste. To help people to do practical waste disposal. To improve the environment’s waste.
3.2The group will require ₱2000 for our expenses, these will be used for transport, website creation, and other miscellaneous funds
3.3The group will be needing gadgets and access to the internet to do the task.

4.Constraints
4.1We anticipate that the group will finish the plan in 7 days
4.2The group will finish the planned budget in 3 days
4.3The planned time for the particular task is per 7 days

5.Success Indicator
5.1The group will know if the assigned task is finished when the plan is already improved and ready for implementation to the Alangilan, Batangas City
5.2The group will know if the task is completed if the group has a carefully budgeted plan for the planned expenses of the group
5.3The group will know if the task has been accomplished if our website results in having no bugs or errors and has successfully informed and educated the community regarding proper waste management and disposal.

The relevant UNSDG and its Importance to our capstone project
SDG 11 is relevant to our CAPSTONE project as SDG 11 and our project has the same goal. Our goal is to make Alangilan cleaner, more sustainable, and a better place for its community members. While SDG 11’s goal is to make cities more inclusive, safe, sustainable, and resilient
